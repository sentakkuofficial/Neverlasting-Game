# Neverlasting: Origins — Mini RPG

A browser-based RPG inspired by my original novel **Neverlasting: Origins**.

Step into the shoes of **Ichido**, a young contestant trapped within the deadly Grand Selection. Fight enemies, survive brutal encounters, and push forward through a world where only the strongest make it out alive.

---

## Story

Ichido awakens in a forest with a red band wrapped around his wrist.

Around him, hundreds of contestants fight for survival in a ruthless tournament known as the **Grand Selection**.

Armed only with his copper chain and his determination to survive, Ichido must overcome deadly opponents and confront the shadows of his past.

Every battle brings him one step closer to uncovering the truth behind the tournament.

---

## Features

- ⚔️ Turn-Based Combat
- ⛓️ Copper Chain Attacks
- 🛡️ Defensive Combat Options
- 🎲 Risk vs Reward Gameplay
- 👤 Multiple Book-Inspired Enemies
- 📖 Story-Driven Progression
- 🌐 Playable Directly in Browser
- 🚀 Easy Deployment with GitHub Pages

## Enemies

### Band Hunter

### Iron-Leg Fighter
A dangerous opponent whose metal-enhanced body makes him difficult to defeat.

### Vidar's Enforcer
A powerful warrior connected to the mysterious man who changed Ichido's life forever.

## Controls

| Action | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Chain Strike | Reliable attack with moderate damage |
| Defend | Reduce incoming damage |
| Risky Power Attack | High damage but may miss |
| Restart | Start a new run |
